== 🗣 Speak Romanian from day one
This is not another grammar-heavy course.
We teach you to **speak Romanian naturally** by using the same sentence patterns native speakers use every day.

**Our method combines:**

- **Sentence-building algorithms** → Easy-to-apply structures for any topic.
- **Vocabulary in collocations** → Words always in natural phrases, never isolated.
- **Repetition out loud** → Build fluency without translating in your head.

== 🎯 Who this course is for

- Absolute beginners learning Romanian online.
- Travelers preparing for a trip to Romania or Moldova.
- Expats living in Romanian-speaking regions.
- Language learners who want to **think in Romanian**, not just translate.

== 📚 What you will learn

- Conversational Romanian for real-life situations.
- Correct grammar without memorizing rules.
- Over 1,000 words and phrases in context.
- How to build your own sentences from day one.

== ⚙️ How it works

. Select a topic you need now.
. Learn 1–2 sentence algorithms.
. Practice with **dialogues and exercises**.
. Repeat out loud until it feels natural.

== 🔥 Popular lesson topics

* 🛒 Shopping — from street markets to supermarkets
* 🍽 Eating out — ordering food, asking for the bill, tipping
* 🚌 Transport — buying tickets, asking for directions
* 🏠 Renting a home — viewing apartments, signing contracts
* 💬 Everyday conversation — greetings, small talk, politeness

== 💡 Why our method works

- You speak in complete sentences from your first lesson.
- Vocabulary is learned in **real, useful combinations**.
- Practice is active — you speak, not just read or listen.
- Clear, **bilingual translations**: Romanian + phonetic transcription + English.

== 📝 Example

* _Algoritmi + lexic + repetiție cu voce = fluență_
* _Algorithms + vocabulary + repetition out loud = fluency_
